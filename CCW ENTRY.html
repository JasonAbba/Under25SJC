COLLEGE CREATOR WEEK

SUBMIT TO CCW

Mellow Monday:


Josephites are known for their vocal chords, mad instrumentals and rad combos of various genres of music.
Through 'Mellow Mondays' we wish to give all budding college creators in the field of music an additional platform to display how fab they are!

Flexin Tuesdays:


Channeling endless energy in the right direction is a talent in itself and that is exactly what these crazy creators do.
Here we wish to give our college creators indulging in dance, fashion, fitness and yoga, an additional platform to display how fab they are.

Artsy Wednesday:


Your brushes are one of your favourite companions, your iPad is your buddy and your imaginative colors know no bounds. You will not share stationary but will love your art like no other.
Throught 'Artsy Wednesday' we wish to give our college creators indulging in visual art of any kind an additional platform to display their talent!

Pen it Thursday:


You write like a dream, your words touch hearts and your thoughts being penned down is one of the little joys in your life and you want to share that with us.
Through 'Pen It Thursdays' we wish to give our college creators who write poetry, motivational paragraphs and short stories an additional platform to display how rad they are.

Visual Friday:


Love the lens? This is for you, who don't fail to be enthralled by life's beauty and you endlessly strive to put out your best capture of it and we want to see more.
Through 'Visual Fridays', we wish to give our college creators indulging in photography and videography an additional platform to display how fab they are!!

Surprise Saturday:


Unnatural talent or something out of the box that will blow our minds? This segment is for you.
'Surprise Saturdays' are for those creators doing something extraordinary and wackiest things possible. Basically... Surprise us.
